Math Can be Fun, Sometimes...

Each of the links above will take you to a app or an animation that is designed to illustrate or demonstrate some part of the math curriculum to you.

You should play around with these to get a sense of how things work. Don't worry about the textbook definitions and formulas just yet. Just try to understand what is happening and how it is behaving.

Then, hopefully, the formal definitions the textbook is giving us will make more sense.
